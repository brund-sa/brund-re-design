The Opera national du Rhin (OnR) is a lyrical and choreographic institution of international reputation.The singularity of their website relift lies on the 1972 merger of the Strasbourg, Mulhouse and Colmar venues. Both the menu and agenda have this triptych, which laid the heart of our ergonomic and strategic thinking. Technically, we chose to outsource the “business” data within a REST API that serves as a middleware between the editorial site - supported by our CMS Roadiz, and the different ticketing platforms. This tailor-made API allowed us to offer a long-term management solution and to help the OnR consider the future of their site serenely.
